Overview:

The price itinerary use case calculates the cost of the selected itinerary by pricing each segment and adding the prices together. This use-case can only be executed from other use-cases. Therefore no controller is provided, a facade is provided that other subsystems may use to price a prepared itinerary. The sequence of pricing an itinerary is captured in one diagram.
